Alert

Closure of the Cambridge with Fenland and Uttlesford Cruse Volunteer Management System


25th May 2022

The Cambridge with Fenland and Uttlesford Cruse Volunteer Management System is no longer in operation. This is due to components of the VMS reaching their end of life.


All user data has been permanently deleted from the system.


Thank you for being a user of this site over the past 4 years.
